Paws for the Cause

Executive Summery

Paws for the Cause is a web application that connects potential pet adopters to pets from shelters in their area. We implement a Tinder like "Swipe" to give users a seamless adoption process experience and the information to allow users to make a well informed decision when adopting a pet.
